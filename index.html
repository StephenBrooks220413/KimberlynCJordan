About Me.

Full Stack Web Developer. Registered Nurse. US Navy Veteran.

I am a Registered Nurse. I decided to learn coding for the challenge and to add a new skill to my tool kit. Not surprisingly I found that I really enjoy learning to program computers and working on coding projects. I started to major in Computer Science right out of high school, but instead opted to pursue healthcare. I have two healthcare degrees. A Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology from the University of Texas Health Science Center Houston and a Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Chamberlain College of Nursing, Houston campus.

January 30, 2020, I decided to teach myself coding. Over the next couple of months I realized I was not making much progress with this goal and decided that the structure of a coding boot camp would keep me on track and enhance my learning experience. I enrolled in the MIT (Massachusetts Institute of Technology) Professional Certificate in Coding course which teaches Full Stack Development with the MERN stack (MongoDB, ExpressJS, React, NodeJS).
